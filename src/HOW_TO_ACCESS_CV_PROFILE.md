# How to Access the CV-Integrated Profile Section

## Location
The CV-Integrated Profile Section is located in the **Job Seeker Dashboard** under the **Profile** tab.

## Steps to Access:

### 1. **Login as a Job Seeker**
   - Click on **"Login"** or **"Sign Up"** button in the header
   - Select **"Job Seeker"** as your role
   - Complete the authentication process

### 2. **Navigate to Dashboard**
   - After logging in, click on **"Dashboard"** in the header navigation
   - Or you will be automatically redirected to the dashboard after login

### 3. **Go to Profile Tab**
   - In the Job Seeker Dashboard, you'll see several tabs at the top:
     - Overview
     - Saved Jobs
     - Applied Jobs
     - Recommended
     - **Profile** ‚Üê Click this tab
